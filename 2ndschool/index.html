Who we are

Second School (2nd School) is a mobile education initiative dedicated to bringing high-quality learning experiences to lower-income communities. Through innovative programs like science buses, community garden setups, and a team of passionate traveling instructors, 2nd School delivers hands-on, engaging education directly to the kids who need it most.
