Volunteer

Lend your hands. Share your heart. Help bring learning to life. At 2nd School, volunteers are the engine behind our mission. Whether you're planting gardens, setting up a mobile lab, or sparking curiosity in young minds—you’re making a direct impact where it matters most. Volunteer Commitment: Whether you can give one hour a month or one day a week, we’d love to have you on board. Opportunities are flexible and designed to fit your schedule.

👐 Ways to Volunteer

🌱 Garden Crew Help us build and maintain educational gardens in local communities.
🚍 Science Bus Support Join us on a bus visit! Assist with setup, student engagement, and cleanup.
📚 Teaching Assistants Support our traveling instructors during lessons and activities.
🎨 Creative & Admin Help Design flyers, write content, or help with event planning behind the scenes.



✅ How to Get Started

  1. Fill out the Volunteer Interest Form
  2. Attend a short orientation (virtual or in person)
  3. Get matched with opportunities near you

    4. That’s it! We’ll provide training, support, and everything you need to jump in.

Questions?

Reach out to our Volunteer Coordinator at volunteer@2ndschool.org or call (123) 456-7890. We can’t wait to have you on the team.
