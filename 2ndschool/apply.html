Apply for your school: Bring 2nd School to Your Community

Want 2nd School to visit your neighborhood, school, or community center? We’d love to hear from you! We’re always looking to partner with communities who believe in expanding educational access and bringing hands-on learning to where it’s needed most. Whether you're a school administrator, community leader, nonprofit organizer, or a parent who cares—here’s how to apply:

How to Apply

Step 1: Complete Our Interest Form: Start by filling out our quick Community Interest Form below. This helps us get to know your location, needs, and the students you serve.

Step 2: Share Your Story: Tell us a little about your community. What kind of impact would 2nd School make in your area? What challenges are you facing, and how can we help?
Step 3: Choose a Program Let us know what type of programming you’re most interested in, You can request one—or all three!:

  1. Science Bus Visit
  2. Community Garden Build + Education Series
  3. Traveling Instructor Sessions
You can request one—or all three!

Who can Apply?

We work with:

  1. Title I schools and districts
  2. Community centers and shelters
  3. Nonprofits focused on youth or education
  4. Grassroots leaders and neighborhood coalitions
  5. Anyone serving kids in lower-income or under-resourced communities
